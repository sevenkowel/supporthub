# Account Verification (KYC)

In accordance with international anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, all customers must complete identity verification (Know Your Customer) before depositing and trading.

## Required Documents

### Personal Account

| Document | Requirement | Format |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Identity Proof | Valid ID / Passport / Driver's License | JPG/PNG, less than 5MB |
| Address Proof | Utility bill / Bank statement within 3 months | JPG/PNG/PDF, less than 5MB |

### Corporate Account

In addition to the above, you also need to provide:

- Corporate registration certificate
- Company charter
- Director identification
- Corporate address proof

## Submit Verification

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **"Account Settings" → "Identity Verification"**
3. Upload the required documents as instructed
4. Submit for review

## Review Time

| Submission Time | Estimated Review Time |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Weekday 09:00-18:00 | 1-2 hours |
| Weekday other times | 4-6 hours |
| Weekend & Holidays | Next business day |

:::tip Speed Up Review
Please ensure the documents you upload are clear and complete:
- All text is clearly readable
- All four corners of the document are visible
- No glare, obstruction, or tampering
- Document is within validity period
:::

## Common Reasons for Verification Failure

1. **Unclear Document** — Please retake or rescan
2. **Document Expired** — Please use a valid document
3. **Address Proof Outdated** — Please provide a statement from the past 3 months
4. **Name Mismatch** — Ensure the name on the document exactly matches your registration name

After verification fails, you can check the specific reason on the verification page and resubmit.
