# Account Registration

Registering a forex margin trading account takes just a few minutes.

## Registration Steps

### Step 1: Fill in Basic Information

Visit the registration page and provide the following information:

- **Email Address** — Used to receive account notifications and trading reports
- **Phone Number** — Used for SMS verification and account security
- **Set Password** — At least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase letters, and numbers
- **Confirm Password** — Both entries must match

### Step 2: Email Verification

After submission, our system will send a verification link to your email:

1. Log in to your registered email
2. Find the verification email from `noreply@forex-help.example.com`
3. Click the **"Verify Email"** button in the email

:::info Verification Email Validity
The verification link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or click "Resend Verification Email".
:::

### Step 3: Choose Account Type

After verifying your email, select the account type that suits your needs:

| Account Type | Min Deposit | Spread | Leverage | Target Users |
|-------------|----------|--------|---------|------------|
| Standard | $100 | From 1.2 pips | 1:100 | Beginner traders |
| Pro | $1,000 | From 0.6 pips | 1:200 | Experienced traders |
| ECN | $5,000 | From 0.0 pips + $3.5/lot | 1:500 | Professional traders |

### Step 4: Set Trading Preferences

- **Base Currency** — USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, etc.
- **Trading Leverage** — Choose based on your risk tolerance (can be changed later)
- **Preferred Platform** — MT4 / MT5 / WebTrader

## After Registration

After account registration is complete, you need to complete **[Identity Verification](/docs/getting-started/account-verification)** before you can start depositing and trading.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I register multiple accounts with one email?**  
A: Yes. You can register up to 5 trading accounts with one email, including different account types.

**Q: What if I don't receive the verification email?**  
A: Please first check your spam folder. If you still haven't received it after 10 minutes, please contact [Live Chat](javascript:void(0)) for assistance.
