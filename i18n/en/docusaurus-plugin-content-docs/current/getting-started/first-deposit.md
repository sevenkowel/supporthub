# First Deposit

After completing identity verification, you can make your first deposit and start trading.

## Before Depositing

- ✅ Account registration completed
- ✅ Identity verification passed
- ✅ Payment method linked

## Supported Deposit Methods

| Payment Method | Min Amount | Max Amount | Fee | Processing Time |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Bank Wire | $100 | Unlimited | $0 (Platform covers) | 1-3 business days |
| Credit/Debit Card | $50 | $20,000 | 2.5% | Instant |
| USDT (TRC20) | $50 | Unlimited | $1 (Network fee) | 10-30 minutes |
| E-wallet (Skrill/Neteller) | $50 | $50,000 | 1% | Instant |
| Local Bank Transfer | $100 | $100,000 | $0 | 1 business day |

## Deposit Steps

1. Log in to **Client Center**
2. Click **"Deposit"**
3. Select your preferred payment method
4. Enter the deposit amount
5. Complete payment as instructed

:::caution Deposit Notes
- Please use payment methods in your own name
- Credit/Debit card payment must match your registered name
- For cryptocurrency deposits, ensure the address is correct; incorrect transfers cannot be recovered
:::

## Deposit Status Check

You can check your deposit status in the **"Transaction History"** page:

- **Processing** — System is processing your deposit request
- **Credited** — Funds have successfully entered your trading account
- **Rejected** — Deposit was rejected; please check the rejection reason
