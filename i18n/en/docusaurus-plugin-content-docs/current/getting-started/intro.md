# Getting Started

Welcome to your forex margin trading journey! This guide will take you through the complete process from account registration to your first trade.

## Overview

Forex margin trading is a financial derivatives trading method that uses leverage to amplify trading scale. By depositing a certain percentage of margin, you can trade currency pairs worth far more than the margin itself.

:::tip Before You Start
Please ensure you are at least 18 years old and understand the basic risks of forex trading. Forex margin trading may result in significant losses.
:::

## Quick Start Steps

1. **[Register Account](/docs/getting-started/account-registration)** — Complete basic information registration
2. **[Identity Verification](/docs/getting-started/account-verification)** — Upload identity documents to complete KYC verification
3. **[First Deposit](/docs/getting-started/first-deposit)** — Choose a payment method to fund your account
4. **[Download Platform](/docs/getting-started/platform-download)** — Install the trading client to start trading

## Core Platform Data

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Tradable Instruments | 50+ forex pairs, gold, silver, oil, stock indices |
| Maximum Leverage | 1:500 |
| Minimum Lot Size | 0.01 Lot (micro lot) |
| Trading Hours | Monday 00:00 — Saturday 05:00 (server time) |
| Execution Type | Market Execution |

:::warning Risk Warning
Forex margin trading carries high leverage risk. You may lose your entire initial margin or even exceed it. Please trade only with funds you can afford to lose and after fully understanding the risks.
:::
